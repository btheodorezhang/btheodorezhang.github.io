My research explores the most energetic phenomena in the Universe, bridging theoretical models with cutting-edge multimessenger observations. By investigating particle acceleration and propagation across both Galactic and extragalactic scales, I aim to decode the origins of the highest-energy particles ever detected.

### Primary Research Areas
* **The Spectrum and Composition of Ultrahigh-Energy Cosmic Rays (UHECRs):** Understanding the sources and survival of ultraheavy nuclei at extreme energies.
* **Galactic PeVatrons & Cosmic Ray Origins:** Utilizing Very-High-Energy (VHE) gamma-rays to pinpoint where Galactic cosmic rays are accelerated to PeV energies.
* **High-Energy Neutrino Astrophysics:** Probing the hadronic interactions inside powerful cosmic accelerators.
* **Gravitational Wave Counterparts:** Modeling the multi-wavelength electromagnetic emissions from compact object mergers.
* **Astrophysical Multimessenger Emission Simulator (AMES):** Core developer of this advanced numerical simulation software for high-energy astrophysics.
